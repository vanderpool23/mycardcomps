About MyCardComps

Welcome to the MyCardComps app, where we determine sports card values using AI technology. Our goal is to revolutionize the sports card industry and provide collectors with the most comprehensive, accurate, and up-to-date comps available.

We understand that finding the right comparable sports cards can be a time-consuming and tedious task, which is why we created this app. Our AI algorithms analyze a vast database of historical sales, market trends, and current conditions to provide you with the most accurate and relevant comps for your sports cards.

With our app, you no longer have to spend hours researching and scouring the internet for comps. Simply input the information about your card and let our AI technology do the work for you. Our app will provide you with a list of comparable cards, their market value, and relevant information about each one.
